📘 How to Use Prompt‑Shree

Prompt‑Shree helps you build effective prompts and instantly get results from ChatGPT with one click.

  1. Enter your prompt in the text area.
  2. Select the GPT model (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4).
  3. Adjust the creativity level using the Temperature slider.
  4. Click Generate to get the output.
  5. Click Optimize to improve your prompt.
  6. Click Save to keep your experiment.
  • Be specific: "Write a poem about sunrise in Haiku style" is better than "Write a poem."
  • Use roles: "Act as a career coach and suggest..."
  • Limit scope: "Summarize this in 3 key points."
  • Iterate: Use the Optimize button to refine.
